# Welcome to kTailor

In this interactive scenario, you will experience **kTailor** – a lightweight Kubernetes Mutating Webhook.

While you are reading this, we have already spun up a real Kubernetes cluster in the background, generated TLS certificates, and installed the official `katalytic/ktailor:latest` image. 

You can focus entirely on the magic of dynamic deployment mutations!

Wait a moment until the terminal on the right says "Cluster is ready!", then click **Start**.
